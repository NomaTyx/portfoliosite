Knockback

This is the game I made as my final project at Vancouver Film School. It was the largest project I'd ever handled by far. When I look through the code with years of hindsight, I think I must have been an utter lunatic. However it taught me a lot about making a project of that scale, and it's helped me start to get a sense for what sorts of things will have knock-on effects later on in a project.
